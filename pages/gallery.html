About Me

My name is Abby Dennin and I have been fostering kittens since 2018. Currently I have successfully fostered and adopted out over 30 kittens. I created this website to spread knowledge about the fostering process and of course share some adorable kitten pictures. I hope to encourage more people to foster kittens and provide information for those who are interested.
